About us

J Campos and Construction is a full-service landscaping company located in Stirling, NJ. Our business is build on honesty, integrity and hard work. Ask any of our customers and they will tell you we go out of our way to make sure our work is done right. We provide free estimates and we are fully insured. No job is too small or to large for us. If you want the work done right by a company you can trust, we are the right fit for you. Our customers become our friends, most of our work is from referrals and customers who call us back for new jobs.

Our services include:

  • Lawn care and Maintainence
  • Patios, Driveways and Decks
  • Snow Plowing and Removal
  • Retaining walls, pool digging, fencing
  • Tree removal, maintainence and planting
  • Fall and Spring Clean-ups
  • Construction services
